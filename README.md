# projects
Data Science Projects (Jupyter Notebooks and R Markdown files)

This repo is devoted to various CUNY School of Professional Studies Master's Program final jupyter notebooks or R markdown files.

Under the `projects` folder, a sample of data science projects are broken out by category:

Regression:

* Underlying Drivers of Total Cholesterol
    *   Collaborated with 2 peers on a multiple linear regression model using R to examine the relationship between cholesterol level and key predictors including demographic characteristics, socioeconomic status, behavioral factors, and clinical measures using the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES)
    *   View in browser: https://rpubs.com/datanerddhanya/1380795

* House Price Prediction
    *   Performed extensive EDA of a dataset consisting of features of residential homes in Ames, Iowa to produce a random forest machine learning model that predicted the final sale price with 96% of the variance explained (R-squared)
    *   View in browser: https://rpubs.com/gillianmcgovern/1406337

Network Analysis:

* Amazon Music Customer Co-Purchase Network Analysis
    *   On a team, utilized 130k Amazon reviews, including user-item/bought together graphs, for 70k albums to analyze Amazon product influence and customer behavior through co-purchase networks and NLP-based product analysis
